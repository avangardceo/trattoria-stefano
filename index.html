Our Story

From the Cobblestones of Rome to the Shores of Da Nang

Stefano grew up in Rome, in a family where Sunday lunch lasted three hours and the amatriciana was non-negotiable. He learned to cook watching his grandmother — her hands dusted with flour, her ragù simmering since dawn. Food, for Stefano, was never just fuel: it was language, memory, love.

After years cooking across Italy and Southeast Asia, he fell for Da Nang — the warmth of the sea, the energy of the streets, and people genuinely hungry for something real. In 2023, he opened Trattoria Da Stefano in the heart of An Thuong: a small corner of Rome, honest and uncompromising.

"Every dish that leaves my kitchen carries a piece of that Sunday table. Come as a stranger, leave as family." — Stefano
